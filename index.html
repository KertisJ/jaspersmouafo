Short Bio

I am a computer engineer with an academic background that I find intriguing, having pursued a Master's degree in Computer Civil Engineering from the Université Libre de Bruxelles. Prior to that, in 2019, I earned a Master's in Information Systems and Software Engineering from the Institut Universitaire de la Côte in Douala, Cameroon.

My passion for computer science extends beyond my education. In 2018, I co-founded a startup that transitioned into a simplified joint-stock company by 2020. Moreover, as an intern, I contributed to platform management at SMALS in 2022, where I adopted an agile work methodology. I also served as a tutor at ADMISTIS, guiding students in their studies in mathematics and computer science.

With every endeavor, my objective remains consistent: to deliver effective and innovative solutions in the field of computer science.

Personal Life

Beyond my passion for computing and my academic journey, I am first and foremost a man of many layers. One of my greatest joys in life is being the father of a wonderful little boy who is 2 and a half years old. Every day with him is a new adventure, a continuous learning experience, and an invaluable source of joy.

Those who know me will say that I'm a cheerful individual, always finding a reason to smile, even in the darkest moments. My sense of humor is contagious, and I like to believe that I often manage to brighten the day of those around me. I cherish the simple pleasures of daily life and firmly believe that laughter is the best medicine.

Besides that, I enjoy spending time with my family, discovering new places with my son, and building memories that will last a lifetime. Family is my anchor, my source of inspiration, and the driving force that pushes me to always give my best, whether in my professional or personal life.

Hobbies and Passions

When I'm not immersed in the digital world, I find refuge and inspiration in cooking. It's an art that brings me as much satisfaction as crafting the perfect code.

To relieve stress and rejuvenate, I lace up my running shoes for a refreshing run through the city. Basketball is another passion where I can unleash my competitive spirit.

The captivating rhythms of salsa and bachata allow me to escape, while the gentle sound of my piano soothes me. And when I feel the urge to create something tangible, I turn to ceramics, molding and shaping clay to bring my ideas to life.
